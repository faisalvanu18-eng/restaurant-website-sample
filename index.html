Our Story

A Royal Table, Rooted in India

Mehranghar Royale brings the depth of Indian cuisine to a refined modern experience — rich gravies simmered slowly, tandoor classics finished with finesse, and spices treated like art. Every plate honors tradition, crafted for today’s elegant palate.

Meet the Chef

Chef Arjun Rathore blends family heritage with contemporary plating — focusing on balance, aroma, and a royal finish. Expect signature marinades, authentic dum techniques, and spice-forward storytelling.
